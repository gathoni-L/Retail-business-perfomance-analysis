# Sales, Customer and Inventory Dashboard

## Overview

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze sales, customer, product, and inventory data. The dashboard integrates multiple datasets to provide business insights that support decision-making in sales performance, customer behavior, and stock management.

The report combines data from sales, customers, products, and inventory tables to create a centralized view of business performance.

## Introduction

The purpose of this dashboard is to transform raw transactional and inventory data into meaningful insights that can be used to monitor performance and identify opportunities for improvement.

The dashboard focuses on key business metrics such as:

* Total Revenue
* Total Quantity Sold
* Average Spend per Customer
* Total Stock Value

Interactive filters were included to allow users to explore data by:

* Product Category
* Supplier
* Customer Membership Status

This approach enables stakeholders to perform quick analysis and make data-driven decisions.



## Findings

The dashboard analysis revealed several business insights:

### Sales Performance

* Revenue and sales quantities vary across product categories, highlighting differences in demand.
* Monthly sales trends provide visibility into performance patterns and potential seasonality.

### Customer Insights

* Customer membership status influences revenue contribution.
* Average spend per customer helps evaluate customer value and purchasing behavior.

### Inventory Insights

* Total stock value provides an overview of inventory investment.
* Product-level analysis helps identify fast-moving and slow-moving products, supporting inventory management decisions.

## Conclusion

This dashboard demonstrates how business intelligence tools can be used to convert raw operational data into actionable insights. By integrating sales, customer, and inventory information into a single interactive report, organizations can monitor performance more effectively, identify trends, and improve strategic decision-making.

The project highlights the value of data visualization and analytics in supporting business growth and operational efficiency.
